"What should I cook?" "How do I cook that?" -- HOME OF FOODIES have an answer for you! HOME OF FOODIEs is here to better your culinary options. This recipe app provides sets of instruction used for preparing and producing a certain food, dish, or drink. The purpose of this app is to keep a precise record of the ingredients used, the amounts needed, and the way they are combined.
